About Me




I am a fourth-year PhD student at Brown University in Providence, RI, USA under Professor Benjamin Kimia. My research focuses on hierarchical approaches to proximity graph search and construction with an emphasis on exactness. I received my undergraduate degree at Roger Williams University in Bristol, RI, majoring in Electrical Engineering and Applied Mathematics.
